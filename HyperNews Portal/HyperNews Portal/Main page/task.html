Description

For now, the visitors can access news only via direct links with the identifiers known only by you. Let's collect the news on one page to show them all to our users. There will be catchy titles, of course: we have to make sure our readers will want to open the full text!

Objectives

At the top of your main page there should be a page header:

<h2>Hyper news</h2>

Below, you need to add some information about your news: the publication date, the title, and the link to its page.
Group them by date into unordered lists:

<h4>2020-02-02</h4>

<ul>
  <li><a target="_blank" href="/news/9234732/">The birth of the star</a></li>
  <li><a target="_blank" href="/news/2389742/">The raise of Jupyter</a></li>
</ul>

The most recent news should be at the top.

If you start the application on your computer with the python manage.py runserver command, the main page will be available at the localhost:8000/news/ address.

Add the link to create a future page with target="_blank" href="/news/create/" below.
